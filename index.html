Finance Terms Explained
APR — Annual Percentage Rate
The yearly cost of borrowing. Lower APR = cheaper deal. Used car PCP is typically 6–12% with good credit.
PCP — Personal Contract Purchase
Deposit + monthly payments for 2–4 years, then a balloon GMFV payment if you want to own it. Most popular UK car finance type.
HP — Hire Purchase
Higher monthly payments but you own outright once all are made. No balloon payment. Build equity from day one.
Section 75 Protection
Pay any part of £100–£30,000 by credit card and the card provider is jointly liable for issues with the seller.
Outstanding Finance Risk
If a car still has finance owed the lender holds a legal interest. Always run an HPI check before buying.
Voluntary Termination (VT)
Once you've paid 50% of total amount payable on PCP/HP you can return the car without further payments (Consumer Credit Act).
