Quick Reference
Generally Legal
Alloy wheels, lowering springs, performance exhaust (if not decat), cold air intake, sports filter, window tint (front ≥70%), tow bar, short shifter, bucket seats.
Declare to Insurer
ECU remap, coilovers, big brakes, LSD, intercooler, turbo, supercharger, anti-roll bar, sports clutch, carbon bonnet, body kit, wrap.
Illegal on Public Roads
Decat pipe (fails MOT emissions), nitrous oxide, roll cage without FIA cert, window tint under 70% front.
